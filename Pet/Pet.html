Labrador Retriever

The Labrador is loyal, obedient and playful. It was bred as a sporting and hunting dog, but is widely kept as a companion dog. It may also be trained as a guide or assistance dog, or for rescue or therapy work.

Boxer

Boxers are a bright, energetic, and playful breed and tend to be very good with children.They are patient and spirited with children, but also protective, making them a popular choice for families.

Golden Retriever

The Golden Retriever is a Scottish breed of retriever dog of medium size. It is characterised by a gentle and affectionate nature and a striking golden coat. It is commonly kept as a pet and is among the most frequently registered breeds in several Western countries.

Siberian Husky

The Siberian Husky is a medium-sized working sled dog breed. The breed belongs to the Spitz genetic family. It is recognizable by its thickly furred double coat, erect triangular ears, and distinctive markings, and is smaller than the similar-looking Alaskan Malamute.

Cocker Spaniel

The cocker spaniel is definitely one of the most handsome breeds, and perfect for first time dog owners as they are relatively easy to train and can adapt to most living conditions. On top of this, they are loving and joyful with a glorious head of hair.
